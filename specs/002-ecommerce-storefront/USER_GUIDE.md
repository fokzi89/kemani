# Kemani Storefront User Guide

## Overview
The Kemani Storefront is your online shop, allowing customers to browse products, place orders, and track deliveries. It integrates seamlessly with your Kemani POS system.

## 1. Browsing & Shopping
- **Product Catalog**: Browse products by category or search by name.
- **Product Details**: Click on any product to view details, variants (size/color), and specifications.
- **Add to Cart**: Add items to your cart. The cart persists for 7 days.
- **Branch Filtering**: If the store has multiple locations, use the sidebar filter to select a specific branch for local availability.

## 2. Checkout Process
### Guest Checkout
- No account required.
- Enter your phone number and delivery details.
- Pay via Paystack (Card, Transfer, USSD).
- Receive an Order ID to track your status.

### Authenticated Checkout
- Sign in with Google, Apple, or Email (Magic Link).
- Save delivery addresses for faster checkout.
- View order history in your account dashboard.
- **Automatic Linking**: If you checkout as a guest using an email/phone linked to an account, the order will automatically appear in your history when you sign in.

## 3. Order Management
- **My Orders**: Log in to view past and current orders.
- **Order Status**: Real-time updates (Pending -> Confirmed -> Out for Delivery -> Delivered).
- **Track Order**: Guest users can track orders using their Order ID or Phone Number.

## 4. Live Chat & AI Support
- **AI Agent**: clicking the chat widget connects you to our AI assistant.
- **Capabilities**:
  - Answer questions about store hours, policies, and products.
  - **Image Recognition**: Upload an image of a product to ask if it's in stock.
  - **Handover**: Type "talk to human" to switch to a live support agent.
- **Attachments**: You can upload images or PDFs (e.g., prescriptions) directly in the chat.

## 5. Security & Privacy
- **Secure Payments**: All payments are processed securely via Paystack.
- **Data Privacy**: Your personal information is encrypted and protected.
- **Account Control**: You can manage your profile and request data deletion.

## 6. Offline Support (PWA)
- **Install App**: You can install the store as an app on your phone (Android/iOS).
- **Offline Mode**: Browse previously visited pages and view your cart even without internet connection.

## 7. Hibernation (For Store Owners)
- If a store becomes inactive for 90 days, it automatically enters "Hibernation Mode" to save resources.
- Owners will be notified before this happens.
- Log in to your Owner Dashboard to reactivate.
